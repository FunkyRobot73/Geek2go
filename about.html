While most kids my age had an ATARI 2600 I had a Commodore VIC-20. Eventually I moved up to a Commodore 64 and it seems I've been playing with Technology ever since. Anybody could buy a game cartridge and play Qbert. I guess that's fun. I preferred to create my own version of Qbert & customize the colors and backgrounds. I preferred to modify and upgrade my RAM & Hard Drive so I could load the latest Star Wars video game even faster. That's the fun stuff.

I first opened up my little computer shop in Etobicoke in June 1998. 25 Years later I can honestly say I have done it all. I've forgotten more than most people ever know about IT & Web Development. If I didn't do it already... I can definitely figure it out.
